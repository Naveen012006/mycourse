TN ELECTION

Results

The 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election results became a historic turning point in the state’s politics. C. Joseph Vijay led Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK) emerged as the single largest party by winning 108 seats out of 234 constituencies. Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) secured 59 seats, while All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (AIADMK) won 47 seats. The election recorded one of the highest voter turnouts in Tamil Nadu’s history, with around 85% participation from voters across the state. The results reflected a major political shift, as TVK’s strong performance ended decades of dominance by the traditional Dravidian parties. Political analysts described the election as a new era in Tamil Nadu politics because of the rise of youth support, digital campaigning, and demand for fresh leadership. After the results were announced, celebrations took place in many parts of Tamil Nadu, with supporters gathering for rallies, public meetings, and victory events. The election outcome also created huge discussions on social media, television channels, and newspapers, where people debated the future development of the state under the new political leadership.
