TN ELECTION

Parties

Many political parties participated in the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election. Some were major state parties, while others were national and regional parties that contested individually or through alliances. Important parties that participated in the election include:

  1. Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK)
  2. Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK)
  3. Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (ADMK)
  4. Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)
  5. Indian National Congress (INC / Congress)
  6. Naam Tamilar Katchi (NTK)
  7. Pattali Makkal Katchi (PMK)
  8. Desiya Murpokku Dravida Kazhagam (DMDK)
  9. Makkal Needhi Maiam (MNM)
  10. Viduthalai Chiruthaigal Katchi (VCK)
  11. Communist Party of India (CPI)
  12. Marumalarchi Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (MDMK)
  13. Indian Union Muslim League (IUML)
  14. Manithaneya Makkal Katchi (MMK)
  15. Kongunadu Makkal Desiya Katchi (KMDK)
  16. Tamilaga Valvurimai Katchi (TVK - Velmurugan party)
  17. Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP)
  18. Tamilar Desam Katchi
