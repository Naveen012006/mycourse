TamilNadu Result 2026

The Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 2026 results created a huge political change in the state. Actor-turned-politician Vijay and his party TVK emerged as the single largest party in the election.

About the Tn election

The 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election became one of the biggest political turning points in the state’s history. For the first time in many decades, the traditional dominance of DMK and ADMK was seriously challenged by a new party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK) , led by actor-turned-politician Vijay.

Election Details

  • Election Date: 23 April 2026
  • Result Date: 4 May 2026
  • Total Assembly Seats: 234
  • Majority Mark: 118 seats

Main Parties

  1. TVK (Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam)
  2. DMK (Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam)
  3. ADMK (Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam)
  4. NTK (Naam Tamilar Katchi)
  5. Congress
  6. BJP
  7. PMK

Final Result

TVK emerged as the single largest party in its very first Assembly election. The party won around 108 seats, while DMK and AIADMK secured fewer seats compared to previous elections.
